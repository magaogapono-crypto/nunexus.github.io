Who We Are

We are a student-led team driven by innovation and the goal of solving real-world problems through technology. Our focus is on developing smart, practical solutions that make everyday life more efficient, starting with how people understand and manage their water usage.

Our mission is to create a system that helps households and businesses track, analyze, and improve their water consumption in a simple and accessible way. By combining technology with real-world needs, we aim to build a product that not only saves money, but also contributes to a more sustainable future.

What We Are Building

Aqua-Nexus is a smart water tracking system designed to help households and buildings monitor, analyze, and improve their water consumption. By turning usage data into simple insights, we make it easier to see where water is being used and how it can be managed more efficiently.

Our aim is to make water tracking accessible, understandable, and useful for everyone — not just large systems or industries.
